# FoodLog

tracking calories and macros is a chore. Typing in search bars, adjusting sliders, and choosing from five different database entries for a single apple gets old fast.

That's why I built FoodLog — a conversational, AI-powered food diary. 🍳

Instead of clicking through forms or searching the food item , you just tell the app what you ate in plain English:

 "I had 2 fried eggs, a slice of whole wheat toast, and 200ml orange juice."


 
 -----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------


How it works:


• Parses the text into individual food items

• Estimates realistic calories, protein, carbs, fats, fiber, and sugar

• Displays your daily progress with smooth, responsive dashboards

• Allows you to review and fine-tune portions inline before confirming

• Works completely offline, syncing automatically when you get back on the grid
